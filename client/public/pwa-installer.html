How GeoLasso Works in the App

Once installed, when you scan an NFC chip:

  1. The TRESR app will open (not the browser)
  2. Your location is captured automatically
  3. The scan is recorded with GPS coordinates
  4. You can see all scans on the map

Note: You'll need to grant location permission when first opening the app.
